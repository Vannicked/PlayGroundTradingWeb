Game Info

Playground Trading, a cutting-edge Python-based software, redefines the investment simulation landscape. Drawing on market data from the Bloomberg Terminal, this platform offers users a tailored semi-historical investment experience, pitting them against a variety of trading bots with specific trading strategies. This white paper delves into the system's architecture, component interactions, development milestones, and the rationale behind the technology choices.
